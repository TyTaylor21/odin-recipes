Pasta Bolognese Recipe

Home Ingredients

  • 150g pasta of choice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef
  • whole milk
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Sweat onions, carrots and garlic in a pot
  2. Add ground beef and season
  3. Cook until beef is brown
  4. Stir in the milk and simmer until it mostly evaporates
  5. Add toato paste and tomatoes
  6. bring the mixture to a boil, then simmer on low, until the sauce is thick
